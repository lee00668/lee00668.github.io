Nav
It is the navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website.
Main
Is primary content of the page
Article
Is the tag used for containing independent piece of content. It could be shared as stand alone piece of content and will still make sense.
Aside
Is the secondary content on the page, this information is not part of the main topic of the page and is not required.
Section
Group of related content on the page. A section must have its own heading.
